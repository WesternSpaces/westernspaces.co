Not every housing barrier is about funding or land costs. In Delta, the development approval process itself had become the obstacle — paper applications, spreadsheet tracking, calendar reminders for deadlines. Builders with viable projects were walking away before they started.

> Proposition 123 brought communities like Delta to the table that might not have engaged otherwise.

Each community started from a different point in the housing conversation.

Western Spaces worked with Dynamic Planning + Science over a 12-month engagement to design a fast-track approval program and review the city's unified development code. The program targets 50 to 80% faster processing for qualifying residential projects, paired with an incentive menu — fee waivers, density bonuses, reduced parking minimums — that removes the bottlenecks. A fix that cost the city nothing but required rethinking how it processed applications.
